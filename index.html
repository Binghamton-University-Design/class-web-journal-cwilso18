Portfolio, One Day

Project 1: Riddle Me This

For our first Project, the class was instructed to break into small groups and create Typographic Riddles. BeiBei, Kerri, Lucy, Maya, Yiqin, and I decided to narrow the assignment down to riddles based solely on movies, and our peers soon followed suit. The goal was to guess the title of the movie with as little illustration as possible. The sketches are of "Stuck in Love", "500 Days of Summer", "Into the Wild", "Call Me By Your Name", "Ingrid Goes West", and "La La Land", respectively. Once we landed on a color palette, I chose a few of my sketches to bring into Illustrator. After rejoining with the entire class, we all agreed on a palette and font that fit better with everyone's designs.
